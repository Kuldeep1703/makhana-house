Our Story

हमारी कहानी

Welcome to The Makhana House, nestled in Darbhanga, Bihar—the global capital of Makhana. Our mission is to bridge the gap between hard-working local farmers and consumers worldwide who seek wholesome, nutrient-dense superfoods.

We pride ourselves on our hygienic harvesting and processing. By skipping the middlemen, we ensure that every batch of our makhana retains its earthy, natural freshness and absolute purity.
